Our Products​

Fresh Durian

Durian is a national treasure fruit of Malaysia, often hailed as the “King of Fruits.” Its flesh is golden yellow with a soft and delicate texture. When ripe, the durians naturally fall from the trees. After being collected and carefully selected by farmers, the high-quality durians are sent to processing plants for cleaning, disinfection, packaging, and transportation. We are committed to excellence at every stage and enforce strict quality control to ensure that every durian is of the highest quality.

Frozen Durian

Frozen durian is one of our company’s key core export products. During the production process, the durians are carefully selected by dedicated personnel and then placed into a liquid nitrogen freezer, where they undergo rapid freezing at -110 degrees Celsius for no less than 1 hour. The durians, after this freezing treatment, perfectly retain their freshness and flavor.

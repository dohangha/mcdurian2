Company Profile​

MC DURIAN —— is a one-stop durian exporter and durian food manufacturer based in Malaysia. Our orchard, spanning over 2,500 acres, is located in Raub, Pahang, an area renowned as the “Home of Musang King.” Musang King is our company’s core production line. We are committed to providing the highest quality and most authentic Musang King durians to our clients, ensuring that both domestic and international partners have the utmost confidence in our products.

The company was founded by Dato’ Sri Chen Zhenhua, who leads a professional team with over ten years of rich experience in the industry. With extensive business experience and networks, and through collaboration with ADC Enterprises, the largest durian grower in Malaysia, our company’s strength has been significantly enhanced. We are now able to cultivate up to 70,000 Musang King and other varieties of durian trees, with an annual production volume exceeding 5,000 tons, preparing us well for entering the vast international market.

As the demands of the overseas market continue to rise, we have identified the Chinese market as our primary target. With our own durian manufacturing and processing plant, our products have a competitive edge in terms of quality, quantity, and price, which has led to gaining the trust of customers in China.

From harvesting, quality management, and product processing to packaging, we meticulously attend to every detail at each stage of the production process. To provide a more comprehensive one-stop service, we continuously improve and innovate to address the procurement needs of our business partners and overseas clients, ensuring they can confidently engage in long-term collaboration with us.

Carrying our company’s vision and mission, we hope that in the near future, not only the people of Malaysia but also people around the world will have the opportunity to taste the most authentic Musang King durians. We aim to increase the global appreciation and recognition of Malaysia’s king of fruits!
